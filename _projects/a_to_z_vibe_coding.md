I started a new challenge for myself called: A to Z Vibe Coding. This challenge is largely inspired by Alexander Tingström's [30 Days of Vibe](https://30daysofvibe.com/). Be sure to check out his amazing work! I decided to take a slightly different approach: each project title is based on a letter of the alphabet (I might start from a random letter). And at the end, I’m planning to include 4 bonus projects (marked with card suit icons), which will be a bit more challenging than the others.

I really love mixing AI with design and finding creative ways to bring the two together. Through these side projects, I like playing at the intersection of logic and creativity. Check out the website [here](https://a-to-z-vibes.vercel.app/)! Currently I am rather busy, so I've only managed to do two, but I hope to do more during the holiday season!
